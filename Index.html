About Me

Designer & Developer

I am Luisa Leaccott, a digital and multimedia designer specializing in 3D modeling and the construction of environments and characters for video games. I have advanced proficiency in Unity and have worked on various augmented reality and virtual reality projects. Through my portfolio, I can showcase my ability to create immersive virtual worlds and captivating characters. I am passionate about combining my technical skills with my artistic vision to collaborate on exciting projects that push the boundaries of imagination and technology.
